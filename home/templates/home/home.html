Welcome to Robolawyer!

Here you can get help with filling in the application form to the European Court of Human Rights.

Did you know that 82% of the applications are declared not admissible on formal grounds, like not filling the application from correctly, not exhausting all national remedies or sending the application more than 6 months after receiving a final decision from the national courts? Before proceeding, please take the admissibility test to see whether your application can be admitted, and then we will help you with filling in all required fields for your specific case!
