About Us

Just Bot aims to help complainants (and their lawyers) improve the quality of their applications to the European Court of Human Rights; currently many claims are rejected immediately, often for simple mistakes, and only 5% reach the stage of receiving a judgement. In the first phase, the project provides a digital tool in English that automates the standard procedure and provides help along the way. In the second phase, we aim to integrate different languages and algorithms to automate the writing process.
Just Bot is based on a partnership between Tech-R and CELL/NRCCL (Faculty of Law, University of Oslo). We would like to especially thank Lovdata for financial support in the first phase and we are open to new partners joining as the project moves forward.


Tech-R (Technology for Human Rights) is dedicated to finding ways of using technology for the protection and fulfilment of human rights, and this project is an important step in fulfilling our mission.
The team working on this project is led by Lelia Marcau, and Kumar Aman developed the website, Nabil Ouhi designed our logo and the entire graphics of the website, and Dan Costin is working on developing NLP algorithms that will help improve the quality of the applications to the ECtHR.

This project is a pilot for the new innovation lab at the Faculty of Law at the University of Oslo. The lab is hosted by the Centre for Experiential Legal Learning (CELL) (A centre of excellence in education) and the Norwegian Research Center for Computers and Law (NRCCL). The project team is led by Professor Malcolm Langford together with Ph.D fellow Runar Lie.

Andra Matei, the leader of Avant-Garde Lawyers and former lawyer at the European Court of Human Rights, undertook the legal quality assurance for our digital form.
