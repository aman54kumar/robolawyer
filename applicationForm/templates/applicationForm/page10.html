VERY IMPORTANT

The application form has to be printed out and sent by post to the registrar of the European Court of Human Rights.

Before mailing the form, please make sure to:

  1. Add copies of domestic decisions in chronological order (add page numbers, including in section I. in the required fields. The page numbering can depend on the number and length of the documents involved);
  2. Sign the application in the required fields;

The completed and signed application form should be sent by post to:

The Registrar
European Court of Human Rights
