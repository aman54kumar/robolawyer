Applicant

Individual

This section refers to applicants who are individual persons only. If the applicant is an organisation, please go back to step1 and choose Organisation.

If your application is declared admissible by the Court, your name will appear on the documents related to your case that are published by the court and are publicly available. You can request not to disclose your identity to the public. The request has to be sent as an attachment to the Court and has to present reasons why making your identity public will affect/cause harm to the applicant (or to others?). The court decides whether you are granted anonymity or not; if you are granted anonymity, you will be referred to only by initials.

Organisation

This section should only be filled in where the applicant is a company, NGO, association or other legal entity.
