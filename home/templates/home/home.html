Did you know ?

On average, only 5% of all the applications submitted to the ECtHR are given a judgment.

The majority of cases are declared inadmissible on formal grounds, such as not filling the application form correctly, not exhausting all national remedies or submitting the application later than 6 months after receiving a final decision from the national court.
