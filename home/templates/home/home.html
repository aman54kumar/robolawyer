Did you know ?

An average of only 5% of all the applications submitted to the ECtHR reach the stage of getting a judgement.

The large majority of cases gets declared inadmissible on formal grounds, like not filling the application form correctly, not exhausting all national remedies or sending the application more than 6 months after receiving a final decision from the national courts. Just Bot's digital form will help you with filling in all required fields for your specific case and avoid the most common mistakes!
