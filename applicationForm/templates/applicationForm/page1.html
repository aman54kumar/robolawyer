Prerequisites
  1. Which state is concerned by your application?

  2. What is the subject of your application?

  3. Have you brought your case in the final instance before the highest court competent to deal with the subject matter of your dispute? The competent national courts in Armenia are in principle:
    4. Type of proceeding National court(s)
    For civil and administrative proceedings the Civil and Administrative Chamber of the Court of Cassation (ՀՀ վճռաբեկ դատարանի քաղաքացիական և վարչական պալատ)
    For criminal proceedings the Criminal Chamber of the Court of Cassation (ՀՀ վճռաբեկ դատարանի քրեական պալատ)

  4. Does your application to the Court comply with the six-month rule?
